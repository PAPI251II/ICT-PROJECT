Lemon and Herb Roast Chicken and Vegetables

Ingredients Quantity
Small red-skinned potatoes (quartered) 1 pound
Medium carrots (cut into 1-inch pieces) 2
Celery stalks (peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces) 2
Red onion (cut into 1/2-inch wedges) 1 medium
Olive oil 2 tsp
Kosher salt and black pepper To taste
Bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts 3 (1 ¾ lbs)
Poultry seasoning 1 tsp
Lemon (halved) 1
Fresh parsley (chopped) ¼ cup

Instructions

  1. Adjust oven rack to the top position and preheat to 450°F. Toss potatoes, carrots, celery, onions, 1 tsp olive oil, ½ tsp salt, and black pepper on a baking sheet. Arrange in a single layer.
  2. Roast vegetables until slightly browned and potatoes start softening (about 20 minutes), tossing halfway through. Meanwhile, rub chicken skin with remaining 1 tsp olive oil, sprinkle with poultry seasoning and ½ tsp salt.
  3. Once vegetables begin to soften, place chicken breasts on top and roast until skin is golden brown and internal temperature reaches 165°F (20-25 minutes).
  4. Remove chicken breasts and let rest. Toss roasted vegetables with juice from half of the lemon and parsley.
  5. Remove bones from chicken breasts and slice the meat. Serve chicken and vegetables on plates, with the remaining lemon half cut into wedges on the side.
