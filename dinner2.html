Adobo Recipe

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 2 lbs
Dried bay leaves 3 pieces
Soy sauce 4 tbsp
White vinegar 6 tbsp
Garlic (minced) 5 cloves
Water 1 1/2 cups
Cooking oil 3 tbsp
Sugar 1 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Whole peppercorn 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic. Mix well and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat the oil: In a pot, heat cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Pan-fry the chicken: Once the oil is hot, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.
  4. Add marinade and water: Pour in the remaining marinade (including garlic) and add water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Simmer: Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  6. Finish cooking: Add vinegar and let cook for 10 more minutes without stirring.
  7. Season and serve: Stir in sugar and salt, then turn off the heat. Serve hot and enjoy!
