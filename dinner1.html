Pork Hamonado Recipe

Ingredients Quantity
Pork (belly, shoulder, or hocks, cubed into 2-inch pieces) 1 lb
Onion (chopped) 1 medium
Garlic (minced) 4-5 cloves
Pineapple chunks (with juice) 1 can (20oz)
Soy sauce To taste
Vinegar (apple cider or white) To taste
Water As needed
Bay leaves 2
Peppercorns 1 tsp
Brown sugar To taste
Salt and pepper To taste
Cooking oil 2-3 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Sear the pork: Heat 2-3 tbsp of oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Brown pork cubes on all sides (2 min per side). Set aside.
  2. Saute the aromatics: In the same pot, saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant (about 2 minutes).
  3. Simmer the pork: Return pork to the pot. Add pineapple chunks, juice, soy sauce, vinegar, water, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Stir to combine.
  4. Finish the sauce: Once pork is tender, uncover, increase heat to medium, and stir in brown sugar. Season with salt and pepper. Let sauce reduce slightly for 5 minutes.
  5. Add pineapple chunks: Stir in the reserved pineapple chunks and cook for another minute to heat through.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot over steamed rice. Enjoy!
