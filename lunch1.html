Lemon Garlic Pork Chops with Parmesan Tater Tots

Ingredients Quantity
Frozen Tater Tots 1 pound
Grated parmesan cheese 2 tbsp
Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper To taste
Green beans (trimmed and halved) 12 oz
Unsalted butter 4 tbsp
Finely grated zest and juice of 1 lemon 1 lemon
Extra-virgin olive oil 2 tbsp
Boneless center-cut pork chops (3/4-inch thick) 4 (1.5 lbs)
Garlic (finely chopped) 3 cloves
Low-sodium chicken broth 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven and bake the Tater Tots as the label directs. Transfer to a bowl, add parmesan, season with salt and pepper, and toss. Set aside.
  2. In a medium pot, bring salted water to a boil. Add green beans and cook until tender (about 5 minutes). Drain, transfer to a bowl, and toss with 1 tbsp butter, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Cover to keep warm.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season pork chops with salt and pepper, then cook until browned (about 4 minutes per side). Transfer to a plate and let rest.
  4. Add garlic to the skillet and cook until lightly browned (about 30 seconds). Add lemon juice and chicken broth, cooking until reduced by half (about 3 minutes). Swirl in the remaining 3 tbsp butter. Spoon the sauce over the pork chops and serve with Tater Tots and green beans.
