Pork Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients
Tomatoes
Onion
Fish sauce
Gabi
Radish (Labanos)
Vegetables
Tamarind
Banana or Finger Chili Peppers

Instructions

  1. Wash tamarind: Rinse tamarind pods under cold, running water to remove dirt.
  2. Boil tamarind: Place in a saucepan with 1 cup of water and bring to a boil. Cook for 4-5 minutes until soft.
  3. Mash tamarinds: Use a fork to mash the tamarinds, releasing the pulp.
  4. Strain the juice: Place the tamarind and liquid in a fine-mesh strainer over a bowl. Continue mashing and return some liquid to extract more juice.
  5. Finish the broth: Discard seeds and skins, then pour the extracted tamarind juice into the pot.
