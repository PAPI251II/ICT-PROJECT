B.E.C. Breakfast Bites

Ingredients
2 hash brown patties, cooked and halved crosswise
4 (8-inch) flour tortillas
4 strips bacon, cooked until crispy and broken into pieces
4 large eggs, scrambled
1 oz. extra-sharp cheddar cheese, coarsely grated
2 tsp. olive oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Assemble wraps: Place 1 hash brown in the center of each tortilla. Evenly top with bacon, eggs, and cheese. Wrap tightly, pleating edges, and place pleated side down.
  2. Cook wraps: Heat 1 tsp oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add 2 wraps, pleated side down. Press down and cook until golden brownâ€”about 2 minutes per large side and 1 minute for smaller sides.
  3. Finish and serve: Repeat with remaining wraps. Cut in half and serve with hot sauce if desired.
