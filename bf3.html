Tomato and Feta Baked Eggs

Ingredients
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 small onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, chopped
¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
2 medium tomatoes, diced
¼ teaspoon oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
1 handful fresh herbs (chopped - dill and parsley recommended)
½ cup feta cheese, crumbled
2 eggs

Cooking Steps

  1. Roast the tomatoes: Preheat oven to 450°F. Toss tomatoes with 1 tbsp oil, smoked paprika, and ½ tsp salt and pepper on a baking sheet. Roast until tomatoes release liquid and break down, about 8 minutes.
  2. Prepare the spices: Using a mortar and pestle, crush coriander and cumin seeds until coarsely ground.
  3. Bake the eggs: Sprinkle feta evenly over roasted tomatoes. Create 8 wells in tomatoes using a spoon. Sprinkle ground spices over tomatoes. Bake until feta melts slightly (2-3 minutes). Crack 1 egg into each well and bake until egg whites set (3-5 minutes). Sprinkle with basil.
  4. Prepare the salad: In a large bowl, whisk lemon juice with remaining 1 tbsp oil. Toss greens with ¼ tsp salt and pepper. Serve alongside baked eggs with toast.
