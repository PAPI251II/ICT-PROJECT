Sweet Potato Burrito Breakfast

Ingredients
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
½ sweet yellow onion, finely diced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
1 medium zucchini, diced
8 large eggs
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional spices: chili powder, cumin, etc.
6-8 large flour tortillas
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or preferred cheese)
Optional toppings: salsa, avocado, hot sauce

Sweet Potato Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven: Set to 400°F. Wash sweet potatoes under running water.
  2. Prepare potatoes: Poke holes with a fork, slice in half, and brush skins with olive oil.
  3. Arrange on tray: Place skin-side down on a foil-lined baking sheet, or wrap in foil.
  4. Bake: If whole, bake for 35-60 minutes; if halved, bake for 20-45 minutes.
  5. Season and serve: Let cool, then season with salt, pepper, butter, paprika, and garlic powder.
